🏆 Grand Prize Farming Optimizer

Monte Carlo search over reset strategies — finds how many rounds to push before resetting for the best expected Grand Prizes per token budget.

ℹ️ Simulation logic

Draw mechanic — each round samples one item, weighted by that item's probability among everything still on the board (weighted sampling without replacement). Drawing the Grand Prize ends the run with a free reset. Pool probabilities are editable in the sidebar.

Costs — playing rounds 1‑10 costs 1, 2, 3, 4, 6, 8, 12, 15, 20, 25 tokens respectively (cumulative 75 to clear a full board). Resetting without the Grand Prize costs 15 down to 1 tokens depending on how far you got — the deeper you push, the cheaper the reset.

Strategy sweep — for each candidate "reset after N rounds" strategy, the optimizer runs many simulated sessions under the token budget and reports the average Grand Prizes earned, tokens spent, and efficiency (prizes per token).

Ticket budget — in advanced mode, the token budget is auto-computed from daily missions (44 fixed, 22/day over the 2-day event), free daily tickets (2 fixed), and any purchased packs (5 tiers × 2 days, toggled independently, no bundling requirement). In simple mode you can enter the budget manually instead.

Milestone tickets — as a simulated playthrough's cumulative wishes (orbs opened, persisting across board resets) cross 20 and 100, the corresponding ticket reward (+5, +12) is added directly to that playthrough's remaining budget, extending how much further it can play. The 250/750-wish rewards are informational only (Fire Crystal chests / Fire Crystals) and do not add tickets.
